Option A — Heartbeat Script Design Doc
One of our founders is non-technical. She manages client relationships, BD, and delivery oversight, but she
does not read code or logs. She works from a MacBook.
We want to build a heartbeat script that runs every 30 minutes while her laptop is active and gives her a
short, useful digest of what is happening across our projects and client conversations.
Your job is to write a design doc for this script. Not the code — just a clear description of what it would do.
Keep it to 200 words. Here are the questions your doc should answer:
What should actually be in the digest? What does someone in her role need to know every 30
minutes?
How do you decide what is urgent and what is just informational?
Which tools or MCP servers would you use, and why those over the alternatives?
What were the hardest calls you had to make, and what did you give up?
